Interactive Bot Controls

Bot Commands

Use the prefix ! before every command.

!play [query]
Connects to your voice channel and plays audio from a search query or URL.
!skip
Skips the currently playing track and advances the queue or autoplay.
!menu
Displays the interactive control menu for Play/Pause, Stop, and Playlist management.
!player
Alias for the main menu, providing full control over the current session.
